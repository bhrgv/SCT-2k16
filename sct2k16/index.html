Theme of the Workshop

Soft computing aims at imitating the human mind ability to effectively utilise approximate rather than exact modes of reasoning to ehance machine intelligence. Soft Computing Techniques incorporate imprecision, uncertainty and partial truths unlike traditional computing Techniques such as evolutionary programming,genetic algorithm, fuzzy logic,neural networks etc. have been extensively used for engineering and other highky complex dynamic systems.This workshp aims to provide a forum for exposition of state-of-the-art for different applications

The objective ISTE of the workshop is to acquaint the participants with soft computational techniques for different applications. This workshop has been organised by EEE dept in association with Indian Society for Technical Education chapter to bring together reputed national level ecperts to exchange knowledge in this field for different applications, evolutionary programming techniques. The workshop will be beneficial for students, faculty and researchers
